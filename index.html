Our Patient-Centric Philosophy

Dr. Salik Imam's practice is built on a foundation of patient-centric care, integrating advanced medical techniques with compassionate support. We focus on evidence-based treatments to ensure optimal, long-term outcomes for every individual, prioritising your well-being and recovery journey.

Orthopaedic Trauma & Fracture Care

Complex Trauma Management

Specialising in intricate fractures, polytrauma, and critical limb salvage procedures, including Road Traffic Accident (RTA) injuries, sports injuries, and industrial trauma. Our expertise ensures comprehensive care for even the most severe orthopaedic challenges.

Advanced Techniques

Utilising cutting-edge methods such as Ilizarov fixation and minimally invasive plating, we promote rapid recovery. Our protocols lead to an impressive >95% successful fracture union rate, getting you back on your feet faster.

24/7 Emergency Support

We understand that injuries can happen at any time. Our team provides round-the-clock emergency support for acute orthopaedic injuries, ensuring immediate and expert care when you need it most.

Advanced Joint Replacement Solutions

Comprehensive Joint Services

Offering a full spectrum of joint replacement procedures, including Total Knee Replacement (TKR), Total Hip Replacement (THR), and Shoulder Replacement. We specialise in primary, revision, and partial joint replacements.

Robotic-Assisted Precision

Employing state-of-the-art robotic-assisted surgery systems, such as CUVIS, MAKO, and NAVIO, for unparalleled precision and alignment. This advanced technology enhances surgical accuracy, leading to superior outcomes.

Durable Outcomes

Customised implant selection is crucial for durable results, with typical implant lifespans extending to 15-20 years. We achieve over 98% patient satisfaction, ensuring significant pain relief and vastly improved joint function.
